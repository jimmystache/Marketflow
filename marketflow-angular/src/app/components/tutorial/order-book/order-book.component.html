Order Book

Reading supply and demand in real time.

↕

Depth

How many units are available at each price level. Deeper books mean more liquidity.

⚖

Imbalance

When there are significantly more buyers than sellers (or vice versa), price tends to move toward the heavier side.

🧱

Walls

Large orders that act like temporary support or resistance price rarely breaks through without strong momentum.

Example

A huge buy wall at 99.50 often keeps the price above that level until enough sell pressure absorbs it.
