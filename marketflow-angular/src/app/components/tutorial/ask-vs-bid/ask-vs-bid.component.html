Bid vs Ask

Understanding the two sides of every trade.

BID

Bid Price

The highest price buyers are currently willing to pay. This is what you receive when you sell.

ASK

Ask Price

The lowest price sellers are willing to accept. This is what you pay when you buy.

↑

How Profit Works

Profit comes from buying at the ask and selling at the bid when the spread works in your favor.

Example

If bid = 101 and ask = 100, buying at 100 and selling at 101 gives you a $1 gain per unit.
