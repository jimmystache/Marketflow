Trade History

Reading past trades to predict future moves.

🔴

Selling Pressure

Trades hitting the bid indicate sellers are willing to take the lower price a bearish signal.

🟢

Buying Pressure

Trades hitting the ask show buyers paying the higher price a bullish signal.

⚡

Trade Flow & Momentum

Fast, consecutive trades in the same direction signal strong momentum. Slow, mixed flow suggests indecision.

Example

Five trades in a row at the ask usually means buyers are aggressive and price may push higher.
