Spread

The gap between buying and selling prices.

=

What Is the Spread?

The spread is the difference between the ask (buy) price and the bid (sell) price: Ask − Bid.

↔

Tight vs Wide

Small spreads mean cheaper trades and higher liquidity. Wide spreads signal lower liquidity or higher volatility.

Example

If bid = 100.00 and ask = 100.05, the spread is 0.05 a very tight, liquid market.
