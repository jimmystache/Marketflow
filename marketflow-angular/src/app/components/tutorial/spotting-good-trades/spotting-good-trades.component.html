Spotting Good Trades

Patterns and signals that indicate opportunity.

💵

Spread Capture

Buy at a low ask, sell at a high bid. The wider the spread, the bigger the potential capture but also the higher the risk.

🚀

Momentum Check

Repeated trades hitting the ask signal strong buyer interest. Consistent sells at the bid suggest seller pressure.

💧

Liquidity Awareness

Thin order books cause slippage your order moves the price against you. Always check depth before trading.

A thin order book simply means there aren’t many buy or sell orders available at different price levels. Think of it as a store with very little inventory on the shelves.

Example

If depth is low, even a small order can move the price significantly be cautious in thin markets.
